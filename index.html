About Skill Swap

Skill Swap is a platform where people can exchange their skills with one another. Whether you’re a designer looking to learn coding, a cook wanting photography tips, or a musician interested in marketing, Skill Swap connects you with others looking to trade what they know for what you know.

How It Works

  1. Sign Up / Log In: Create an account or log in to access full features.
  2. Create Your Profile: Add your skills and the ones you're looking for.
  3. Search & Filter: Browse users by category, location, or keyword.
  4. Request a Swap: Found a match? Click “Request Swap” to connect!
  5. Connect & Learn: Chat, collaborate, and grow together.
